Parent Education Analysis

Distribution of Parent Education

Distribution of Parent Education: This pie chart illustrates the educational attainment of students' parents. The data shows that the largest group of parents has some college education, representing about 23% of the total. High school graduates make up approximately 20%, followed by those with associate's degrees (19%). The distribution is rounded out by parents with some high school education (19%), bachelor's degrees (12%), and master's degrees (7%). Understanding this distribution helps to identify the educational background of the students' households, which can influence educational outcomes and support needs.
